At Rancho Dental Group, we strive to provide high-quality dental care in a warm, comfortable environment. Our San Diego dental practice is built on a foundation of trust amongst our staff, doctor, and patients because we understand that it isn’t always easy to feel comfortable at the dentist. We are dedicated to giving our patients the stellar care they need while helping them feel safe, positive, and relaxed.

Our patients’ health and happiness are our top priorities. That’s why we use only the latest state-of-the-art dental technology and modern dental techniques to ensure your optimal care and comfort. We also offer several patient amenities so you can feel at peace during your appointment.

With Dr. Richey, and their staff, you’ll be in good hands at our San Diego dental practice. They can help you achieve the healthy, functional smile you’ve always wanted. We welcome new and current patients alike.
